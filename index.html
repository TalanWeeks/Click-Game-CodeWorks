How To Play

Welcome to Moon Miner! The goal of this game is to become the richest moon cheese miner around. In order to mine cheese you must click on the moon image. Watch out for your bucket filling. You must empty your bucket to add the cheese to your total. If your bucket fills up your cheese will fall out and not count towards your total. Make sure to buy rovers when you're rolling in the cheddar. They empty your bucket so you don't have to.
